    Our Story

    Our Story

    The project was born in 2017 from the recognition of the difficulty that many students had in passing the admission tests in the first choice of university. By providing academic reinforcement in the four basic subjects, we hopped that students would be able to get into the university they wanted to apply to. In addition, we sought to focus the project so that those who participated would achieve good academic results that would allow them to obtain university scholarships and maintain a good performance at this educational level.

    The academic reinforcements began at the ¡Supérate! Poma Foundation, where they were given to third year scholarship students. During the following years, the project continued to be implemented in this center by adding a team of tutors, where in the following years some were students who were part of the project. In 2019 it was extended to the ¡Supérate! Merlet to incorporate more students and have a wider reach. During 2020 the project was put on pause as a result of the COVID 19 pandemic, resuming in 2021 only with the ¡Supérate! Fundación Poma in a virtual way, being a great challenge to change the modality. In the same year, the project won the Sagrera Palomo Foundation Solidarity Award, with which it was possible to acquire equipment and new tools to improve the project's performance.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    In what modality are the classes taught?

    Currently, our academic reinforcement classes are virtual through the ZOOM platform.

    Are there any entry requirements?

    To attend a public high school level and have the desire to reinforce knowledge in basic subjects.

    Where is Edúcate located?

    We do not have a specific location, but we usually go to the ¡Supérate! Fundación Poma to teach classes to the students in the program.
